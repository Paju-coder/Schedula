### **Color Palette**
The design uses a "Light Mode" aesthetic with a clean, professional, and tech-forward feel.
*   **Primary Background:** White (`#FFFFFF`) and a very light off-white/gray (`#F7F7F7`) for section backgrounds to create subtle contrast.
*   **Action/Accent Color:** 
    *   **Orange/Coral:** Used for key emphasis in headlines and small UI elements.
    *   **Black (#000000):** Used for primary CTA buttons and main headings.
*   **Secondary Accents (Pastels):**
    *   **Soft Mint Green:** Security sections.
    *   **Soft Lavender/Blue:** Portfolio and secondary stats.
    *   **Soft Peach/Tan:** Cryptocurrency icons and backup features.
*   **Text Colors:**
    *   **Deep Black/Charcoal:** Headings and primary labels.
    *   **Muted Gray:** Body text and sub-headers.

### **Typography**
Modern Sans-Serif (Inter/Roboto style).
*   **Headlines:** Bold or Extra Bold, tight letter spacing, large X-height.
*   **Body Text:** Regular or Medium weight, clean lines, generous line height.
*   **Data/UI Text:** Monospace or high-clarity Sans-Serif for numbers and prices.

### **Design Themes**
*   **Minimalist & Spacious:** Heavy use of white space.
*   **Card-Based Layout:** Rounded containers (cards).
*   **Soft Geometry:** Rounded corners on buttons, cards, and input fields.
